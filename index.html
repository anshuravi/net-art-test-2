A slip of parchment falls from the glittering chandelier above. A ripped page from the maid's diary reads, "The gateway of endless worlds." Proceed to the library.
Looking around for clues, you spot an ancient book. Hidden underneath the display lies a set of bloody cufflinks engraved with the initials "J.K." Head towards the study.
A table lies in the center of the room. Atop it lies a forged will. In almost exquisite penmanship, it dedicates his late father's property to Jasper Knight II. Reaching forward you pick up the will noticing a crumbled masquerade invitation next to it. Proceed to the ballroom.
The ballroom stands in gleaming perfection ready for the next masquerade. You scan the room, nothing seems out of order. Yet your eyes gloss over to the grand piano, a glint catching them. You head over, picking up a heart shaped locket. The locket clicks open, a picture of a woman inside. Realization hits you, this must be Mrs. Knight, Jasper Knight II's mother. Head to Mrs. Knight's bedroom.
Entering the bedroom, the copper smell of blood enters your nostrils. There, behind a canvas painted in blood the words "The Sins of Son" stain the wall. Proceed to Jasper Knight II's chambers.
In the corner of the room lies a bundled red cloth. Inside lies a dagger stained with blood. Head back to the main foyer.
